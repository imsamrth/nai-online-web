FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about Nai before you get started.

What is Nai? +
Nai is India's smart salon and grooming platform. It helps you discover nearby salons and barbers, compare options, and book appointments easily through a mobile app with no calls needed.
Is Nai available in my city? +
Nai is currently launching across major Indian cities. We're expanding fast. Sign up on the app to be notified when Nai reaches your city, and to get early access when it does.
Can I book appointments through the app? +
Yes, that's one of the core features. You can browse available slots, pick a time, and confirm your booking in a few taps. You'll receive reminders before your appointment.
Does Nai support home services? +
Yes. Nai connects you with verified grooming professionals who offer home services including bridal mehndi, facial, at-home haircuts, and more. Perfect for special occasions or everyday convenience.
Is Nai free to use? +
Absolutely. The Nai app is completely free for customers. Browse, discover, and book salons without any subscription or hidden fee.
How does personalization work on Nai? +
When you set up your profile, you share your preferences like hair type, style choices, preferred budget, and services you like. Nai uses this to surface more relevant salon and barber recommendations over time.
How can my salon partner with Nai? +
Salons and barbers can apply to join Nai through the Partner section. Once approved, you get a full profile on the app, a booking management dashboard, and access to customers searching for your services nearby.

Partner FAQ

How do I list my salon on Nai? +
Simply fill out the contact form below or download the Nai Partner app. Our team will review your application and set up your profile within 48 hours.
Is there a fee to join as a partner? +

What kind of salons can join? +

Our Story

About Nai

Building India's smartest salon discovery and booking platform.

Our Mission

Nai was born from a simple frustration: finding a good salon shouldn't be this hard. In a country of 1.4 billion people, the grooming industry remains largely unorganized, offline, and unpredictable. We're here to change that.

Our mission is to make grooming effortless for every Indian by connecting people with trusted salons and barbers through smart technology, honest reviews, and seamless booking.

What We Believe

We believe everyone deserves a great grooming experience regardless of where they live or what their budget is. We believe salon owners deserve modern tools to grow their business. And we believe technology should simplify life, not complicate it.

Our Approach

Nai is built with deep empathy for both customers and salon partners. Every feature is designed based on real conversations with salon owners, barbers, and customers across India. We don't just build tech. We build trust.

The Team

Nai is built by a passionate team of designers, engineers, and grooming industry experts who are committed to transforming how India experiences personal care. We're based in India and building for India.
